IMPACT SCORE

Consider the size of your home. A smaller home makes a smaller impact on the environment. Take the size of your home into account as you calculate your impact.

If you have a large house your number will be 1 + 10
If you have a medium-sized house your number will be 2 + 7
If you have a small house your number will be 3 + 4
If you have a apartment your number will be 4 + 1
